Artificial Intelligence Bot

A Narrow Artificial Intelligence project I’ve been developing whenever I get bored. It’s now hosted on my inactive TEDCSS Blog just for fun.

It can receive commands such as navigate through pages, search for terms, open blog posts, and much more just by using your voice.

It started as a testing subject, but it’s becoming more and more complex and might actually become a plugin available for purchase one of these days.
At first it was designed to receive commands in English but now it's fully Portuguese because that’s the main language of the Blog in the first place.
